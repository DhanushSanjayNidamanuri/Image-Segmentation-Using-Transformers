This repo consists of our project, Image segmentation using transformers, for the course CS736 Medical Image Computing.
We segmented images of two different datasets (BTCV, DRIVE) , each has slightly different architectures.
To reproduce or view the code, Explore the corresponding folders "btcv" and "drive" each of which contains their own README for further instructions.
To Know about the Theory, Architecture, Results, Have a look at the presentation.pdf
The journal, research paper we used are also present. Have a look at them to understand the reasoning, architecture in detail.
